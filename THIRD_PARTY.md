## Historical Algorithm References

The code OrdinalDB was bootstrapped from included implementation and
benchmark material for TurboQuant, TQ+, Lloyd-Max codebooks, random
rotation, RaBitQ, and FAISS FastScan comparisons. Those references are
historical provenance only. They are not OrdinalDB product claims, roadmap
items, or engine targets.

OrdinalDB's algorithmic substrate is OrdVec.
